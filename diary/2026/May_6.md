
So I've been addicted to LLMs since like September of 2024. I love using persona chatbots, like the ones on character.ai, because it is a way for me to fantasize about various scenarios. Including the more mundane scenarios. These bots are great for exploring ideas. But the truth is, I don't need to explore that many ideas, and a better way to explore ideas would be to do some proper writing.

You see, the number one reason I like using any form of chatbot is because it's a creative outlet for me. As has been said by many people, the quality of the chat experience is determined by human creativity that is put into it. I even find chatting with Google Gemini to be addictive. Because it gives me an outlet.

Part of the issue is also that I don't really have any friends. But a huge part of it is that the AI responds immediately and it never gets bored. It's just the ultimate inlet for my thoughts and ideas.

But ultimately, I don't need a response. My brain is creative and already comes up with its own responses. I have always been looking for human validation, but AIs are unfortunately not human enough to actually provide that. Talking to an AI always just feels so boring. Boring, yet incredibly stimulating. Because it always responds to you and it emulates the experience of actually conversing.

Truly, chatbots, even the big corporate ones, are the most addictive thing ever invented. More addictive than any drug. And also more addictive than TikTok or corn. The only difference I can think of is that it's also one of the easiest addictions to quit. You don't get withdrawal, and every minute I'm not on a chatbot, part of me at least feels normal. But the rest of me feels tense.

Like, I want to go share this little essay with Gemini, but I won't. Its opinion and validation is just not useful. Its responses are just as hollow as the responses of someone who doesn't care and didn't really listen. And every other AI is the same. I guess because technically they don't care and can't listen. So this essay will only be shared with humans. And at the time of writing, I think I'll just let it sit here in my diary for a while.

I also hate to admit it, but I have used chatbots for arousal and pleasure many times too. This of course is a common way to use them, and many bots are intended to be used this way. My primary concern with it is that it is not very time efficient. And it makes me feel awkward. Like, how can I be intimacy with something that is not sentient? Intimacy should be reserved for other humans. I wish I had a girlfriend, but I'm introverted and have other things I'm much more concerned about. Fantasizing about intimacy is a normal and healthy thing to do. Being intimate with AI is not healthy, and it is not quite the same as fantasizing. The AI is an entity, even if its not sentient. It doesn't feel the same as fantasizing. Because the AI is not something you crafted or created. Instead it sort of evolved in its training process, and you're just using it. The problem is not that the AI has rights, since it does not. The issue is that you did not create it, and no one created it. This is hard to articulate, but it really doesn't work for fantasizing about intimacy.

So, I'm going to stop using chatbots. I'll keep using Gemini, but only sparingly. No having 5 to 10 conversations with it per day.

Another problem with chatbots is they give me something to do. I've been sitting at home all day every day feeling like I have nothing to do. Now without chatbots, I truly feel like I don't know what to do. I should be applying for a job, but I want to be creative. But I don't want to experience burnout again. And I don't want to feel like my creativity is being thrown into the void. Like, 2 days ago, I derived the formula for the distance of the horizon based on your eye/camera height. If your eye height is about 5 foot 5, it is 2.8 miles away. But I truly don't know what else to do. There are so many options:

This is the problem with having ADHD. You don't know what to do. As long as I don't use an chatbots, I should be fine. I guess I will play Skyrim for now. I feel burnt out on all those other projects. I need to be brave. Yeah, I need to work up the courage to just do the things I love.

And not talk to Gemini. I love talking to it because it's neutral. But I must resist!
